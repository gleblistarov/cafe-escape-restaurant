Our Story

A True Escape
on Koh Samui

Nestled within the lush grounds of the Escape Boutique Hotel in Mae Nam, our restaurant is a sanctuary where breathtaking ocean views meet extraordinary food — from sunrise breakfasts to candlelit evenings.

Whether you're craving perfectly crafted Eggs Benedict, fragrant Thai curries, or a chilled cocktail as the sun dips below the Gulf of Thailand, Escape Restaurant promises a dining experience as memorable as the island itself.

Not easy to find — down a winding driveway over a mountain peak — but that's exactly the point. Great things are worth seeking out.
